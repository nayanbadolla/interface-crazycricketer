CRAZY CRICKETER

The score card of a cricket game, denoting the runs scored per ball is recorded without leaving any spaces between numbers:

380171246010118910253641618239

Correct the score card if the total no of runs conceded from the four overs is: 166

(Note that the wides are added with the next valid ball (for example, a wide and then a six would be recorded as a 7).

Also with a no-ball the runs are recorded as [runs in no-ball] + [runs in free hit] + [runs in wide] + [runs in the next valid ball]

(for example, 1+6+4=11 if it's a dot no ball + a six in free hit + a boundary in the next ball)
